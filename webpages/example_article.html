A fight against Greenwashing!

At the heart of EcoEval lies a commitment to innovation grounded in real-world data. EcoEval aims to harness insights gathered from comprehensive field research to develop a sustainable solution. This solution stands as a bulwark against greenwashing, the practice of misleadingly portraying a company's products or policies as environmentally friendly. EcoEval seeks to create a secure space where companies can access factual information about their peers and leave comments on the products or services used.

EcoEval's primary purpose is to combat greenwashing effectively. By providing a centralized platform for companies to access authentic information about each other, EcoEval fosters transparency and accountability. This not only serves as a valuable resource for businesses genuinely committed to sustainability but also acts as a deterrent against misleading practices. In essence, EcoEval becomes a hub for promoting honesty and integrity in sustainability efforts.

The website is perfect for people who want to check how genuine a product is. You do not have to pay as a consumer, as you will have a free overview of the companies. It is much more limited than the companies who pay for the services, but it is more than enough to decide if a company lives up to your green standards.

This inclusive approach democratizes access to sustainability evaluations, empowering individuals to make informed choices about the products and services they support. As more consumers adopt EcoEval, the platform becomes a powerful driver for encouraging businesses to uphold higher sustainability standards.
